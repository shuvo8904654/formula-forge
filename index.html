Common Oxidation State Rules

  • Free elements have oxidation state of 0
  • H is usually +1 (except in metal hydrides: -1)
  • O is usually -2 (except in peroxides: -1)
  • Alkali metals (Group 1) are always +1
  • Alkaline earth metals (Group 2) are always +2
  • Halogens are usually -1 (except with O or other halogens)
  • Sum of oxidation states equals charge of species
